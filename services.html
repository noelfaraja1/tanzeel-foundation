The foundation provides different kinds of services to the community which are very important to them.These services are as follows;

Nisomeshe

This deals with distributing school facilities to the Orphans and Vulnerable children who live outside the Orphanage centers which happens once a year.The deputy Mayor of Dar es salaam city council HON.SAAD KHIMJI Distributing stationeries to students in 2021

Ramadhan Package

This deals with distribution of Iftar Packages to the Families that have Orphans, Elderly, Widow, Disabled People who can not afford the Iftar foods and it takes. Members of Tanzeel foundation visited the Orphans and distributed IFTAR PACKAGES at MABWEPANDE VILLAGE IN 2021

Iftar With Orphans

This deals with motivating and enjoying together with Orphans and having the Iftar with Orphans at the Orphanages on every weekend of the Holy Month of RAMADHAN.Mayor of Dar es salaam city HON. OMAR KUMBILAMOTO together with the regional sheikh. ALHAD MUSSA getting iftar together with orphans at ASHURA FOUNDATION ORPHANAGE CENTER in 2021

Eid Gala

This deals with celebrating eid in Islamic manner together with Orphans and fundraising for the provision of aid to community and it usually happens once a year.Celebrating Eid with Orphans in Islamic Manner at CITY GARDEN RESORT K/KOO DAR ES SALAAM.

Wavalishe

This deals with distributing of clothes to the orphans and less privileged people around the villages in Dar es salaam Region and it usually happens once a year.The vulnerable children have received clothes and mashfu from TANZEEL FOUNDATION at MABWEPANDE VILLAGE In 2021.
