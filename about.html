Short History

Tanzeel foundation was established on 01 January 2021 by the Youth who are ready to find the changes in the community through provision of different services to people.

Our Vision

To invest in education and help to solve social, economic and environmental challenges and thus help comunities to be healthy, economically capable and able to solve the challenges they face.

Our Mission

To Build a free and independent society in every aspect of life,and thus being able to overcome ignorance, disease and poverty and create a strong society.

Why donating?

To ensure that the less privilege people in the communities are left in a conducive environment all the time and have a place where they can ask for the help and get through out their life time.
